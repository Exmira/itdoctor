FAQ

Some questions we often get asked.

What types of devices do you repair?

We repair desktop computers and laptops.

What kind of issues do you fix?

We can address a wide range of issues, including hardware failures, software problems, slow performance, networking issues, and data recovery.

How long will it take to repair my computer?

The repair time can vary depending on the complexity of the issue. We strive to provide an estimated turnaround time when you bring in your device or contact our customer service team.

How much will the repair cost?

The cost of the repair depends on the specific problem and the required solutions. We offer competitive pricing and will provide you with a detailed quote after diagnosing the issue.

Do you offer any warranty on the repairs?

Yes, we stand behind our repairs. We offer a warranty period for the repaired parts and services provided. The warranty details will be provided along with the repair.

Can I ship my device to you for repair?

Yes, we offer the option to ship your device to us for repair. Please contact our customer service team for shipping instructions and to arrange the repair process.

Do I need an appointment for a repair?

While appointments are not always necessary, we recommend contacting us in advance to schedule a repair. This ensures that we have the necessary resources available to handle your repair promptly.

What happens if you cannot fix my computer?

In rare cases where we are unable to repair your computer, we will provide you with an explanation and potential alternative solutions.

Can you help with computer upgrades?

Yes, we offer computer upgrade services. Whether you want to add more memory, upgrade your hard drive, or improve your graphics card, we can assist you in enhancing your computer's performance.
